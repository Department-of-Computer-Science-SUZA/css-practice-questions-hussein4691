Emesrson Spice Located right along the coast of Zanzibar’s Stone Town, Emerson Spice has gathered a well-deserved reputation for being one of the most hospitable hotels in the region. A lovingly restored merchant’s house, the hotel is a mix-match of tradition and modernity – a real personification of Zanzibar itself. Aside from being a top place to stay while finding things to do in Zanzibar, Emerson Spice is a fantastic place to enjoy a sunset meal in the old town. The complex is home to a staggering four restaurants, with two of them situated on the rooftop. This setting allows you to sit back and enjoy a lovely evening dinner while watching the sunset over the coastline.With the huge sun dropping behind the Indian Ocean and the mystical call to prayer on the wind, you can dine on a five-course, seafood-based tasting menu, which changes daily.
